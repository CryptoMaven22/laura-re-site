Laura RE

An AtlasFlow real estate workflow that turns lead replies and capture notes into AI summaries, priorities, and recommended next actions.

What Laura Does

Laura helps real estate agents manage messy lead replies and quick capture notes. Instead of leaving important context buried in texts, notes, or memory, Laura structures the information, summarizes what matters, assigns priority, and recommends the next action.

Core Workflow

WhatsApp reply or capture note → AI summary → priority → recommended action → agent review

Use Cases

Lead Reply

Captures a buyer or seller response and summarizes what the agent needs to know.

Agent Capture Note

Turns quick unstructured notes into clean follow-up information and next steps.

Priority Routing

Flags which leads or follow-ups need the most urgent attention.
