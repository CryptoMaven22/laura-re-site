Why It Matters

The work is not about software for its own sake. It is about reducing operational noise and making decision pressure visible.

Faster reviewLess time spent reading, interpreting, and re-stating messy requests.
Clearer decisionsOperators see the situation, risk, and recommended next step in one place.
Fewer missed risksExceptions, handoffs, and readiness gaps become visible earlier.
Better handoffsThe next person sees context without chasing messages or tribal knowledge.
Less operational noiseRoutine clutter becomes structured enough to review quickly.
More visible workPrototype viewers make invisible workflow pressure easier to discuss and improve.
