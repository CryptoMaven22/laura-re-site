The operating loop

The PRD defines the proof as a lead reply coming in, Laura understanding intent, urgency, and recommended next step, then updating the database, notifying the agent, and reflecting the next action in the dashboard.

Lead replies by WhatsApp and the inbound signal is captured.

3

Understand

OpenAI turns the reply into priority, summary, reason, and next action.

4

Notify

The assigned agent receives a concise enriched alert.

5

Show

The dashboard shows the record, status, AI output, and handoff.

What Laura is proving

Laura proves that execution should not depend on agent discipline. When a lead replies, Laura captures the signal, understands what matters, alerts the assigned agent, updates workflow state, and makes the handoff visible.


Current decision lock

Demo channel is Twilio WhatsApp Sandbox. Production consumer SMS remains blocked until A2P approval, while the demo continues through sandbox/internal channels.


Duplicate protection

Re-run protection prevents repeated agent alerts once laura_status equals agent_notified.

AI Assist rules

AI Assist does not conduct autonomous conversations. It clarifies the handoff by turning raw reply text into structured business output.

High

Clear intent

Defined timeline, request to talk, showing/appointment request, or availability.

Medium

Interested but vague

Asks a question, needs more info, or timing is uncertain.

Low

Not ready

Delayed timeline, weak buying/selling signal, or low urgency.

Needs Review

Ambiguous

Unclear, incomplete, emotional, or cannot be confidently categorized.

Roadmap context

The roadmap frames Laura as the lead-conversion core before expanding into Laura Expanded, Carmen, Nancy, and a cross-platform operating layer.

Phase 1 · Laura MVP

Lead intake, first SMS follow-up, inbound reply capture, agent notification, and status tracking.

Phase 2 · Laura Expanded

Qualification workflow, conversation visibility, agent approval controls, dashboard, and stronger automation logic.

Phase 3 · Carmen

Transaction execution, deadline tracking, coordination, issue/risk management, and closing completion tracking.

Phase 4 · Nancy

Post-close follow-up, review generation, referral prompting, and long-term nurture workflows.
