Coordinator Actions

  • Approve only when site, preceptor, and student capacity align.
  • Investigate requests with partial information or capacity uncertainty.
  • Escalate likely conflicts, coverage gaps, or risky assignments.
  • Use Arc output as review guidance, not final authority.
Demo note: Arc uses synthetic data only and is designed for human review. It does not make final scheduling decisions.
